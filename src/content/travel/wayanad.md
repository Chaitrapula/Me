Wayanad sits up in the Western Ghats where Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu all meet. It's coffee and spice country, and in January it wakes up under a thick coat of fog that burns off slowly through the morning.

## Mornings by the river

We stayed at a homestay tucked into the trees, the kind of place where breakfast smells like filter coffee and the only alarm clock is the birds. The best part of each day happened before anything was planned: a walk down to the river while the mist still hung over the water, everything soft and grey and quiet.

## Forest light

Once the sun got above the canopy, the whole forest changed. Light came down through the trees in long beams, catching the dust and the dew. We just walked, in no hurry, letting the trail decide the pace.

- Go in winter for the fog and the cool mornings.
- A homestay on a plantation beats a hotel here, every time.
- Leave the schedule loose. The slow mornings are the point.

> Some trips are about the big sight at the end. This one was about the walk to the river, every morning.
