Kashmir in April can't decide what season it is, and that's the best thing about it. Mornings start cold enough for a jacket. By afternoon the tulips are wide open under a hard blue sky.

## On the lake

The classic thing to do in Srinagar is also the right thing to do: take a shikara out on Dal Lake at first light, before the water gets busy. The lake sits dead still, the mountains double themselves in the reflection, and the only sound is the paddle.

## A hillside of colour

The Tulip Garden below the Zabarwan hills opens for only a few weeks each spring, row after row of colour running down toward the lake. Time your trip wrong and you miss it entirely. Time it right and you can't stop taking photos.

Higher up, the snow still hadn't given way to spring. I found a patch of it and behaved like a child.

> Go for the lake, stay for the way the light keeps changing its mind.
